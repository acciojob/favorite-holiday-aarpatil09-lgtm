Goa


Goa is one of the most popular holiday destinations in India, known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, rich culture, and delicious seafood. It offers a perfect mix of relaxation and adventure.

  1. Baga Beach – famous for nightlife and water sports
  2. Calangute Beach – known as the "Queen of Beaches"
  3. Fort Aguada – historic fort with scenic views
  4. Dudhsagar Waterfalls – breathtaking natural waterfall
  5. Basilica of Bom Jesus – UNESCO World Heritage site
  • Goan Fish Curry – traditional spicy curry
  • Prawn Balchão – tangy and spicy prawn dish
  • Bebinca – famous Goan dessert
  • Chicken Xacuti – rich and flavorful curry
  • Feni – local alcoholic drink
