Paris, France


Paris, known as the City of Light, is famous for its art, culture, and cuisine. Visitors love exploring its iconic landmarks and charming streets.

  1. Eiffel Tower – the most famous landmark in Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – home to the Mona Lisa and other artworks.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – a historic Gothic cathedral.
  4. Sacré-Cœur – located on Montmartre with amazing views.
  5. Champs-Élysées – a vibrant avenue full of shops and cafes.
  • Croissants – flaky and buttery pastries for breakfast.
  • Escargot – cooked in garlic butter sauce.
  • Macarons – colorful sweet treats made with almonds.
  • Baguette – the classic French bread.
  • Ratatouille – a delicious vegetable stew.
