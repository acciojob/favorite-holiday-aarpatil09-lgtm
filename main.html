Paris, France


Paris, known as the City of Light, is famous for its art, culture, and cuisine. With its iconic landmarks, charming streets, and world-class museums, it is a top destination for travelers from around the world.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The most iconic symbol of Paris offering breathtaking city views.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the Mona Lisa and countless masterpieces of art.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A stunning example of Gothic architecture with rich history.
  4. Sacré-Cœur Basilica – Perched on Montmartre hill with panoramic city views.
  5. Champs-Élysées – The famous avenue lined with shops, theaters, and cafes.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – Flaky, buttery pastries that are perfect for breakfast.
  • Escargot – Classic French dish of garlic butter snails.
  • Macarons – Colorful, delicate, and sweet almond-based cookies.
  • Baguette – The traditional French bread with a crispy crust and soft interior.
  • Ratatouille – A flavorful vegetable stew perfect for vegetarians.
