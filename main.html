Paris


Paris is a beautiful city known for its art and culture, attracting tourists from all over the world.

  1. The Eiffel Tower is an iconic landmark in Paris.
  2. The Louvre Museum contains many famous artworks.
  3. The Notre-Dame Cathedral is a historic Gothic cathedral.
  4. The Sacré-Cœur Basilica offers amazing views of the city.
  5. The Champs-Élysées is a lively avenue full of shops and cafes.
  • Try the Croissants for a classic French breakfast.
  • Enjoy Escargot cooked in garlic butter sauce.
  • Sweet Macarons are a must-try dessert.
  • A fresh Baguette is a staple of French cuisine.
  • Delicious Ratatouille is a popular vegetable dish.
